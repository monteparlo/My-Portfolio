About Me

I am the MOST creative girl you will ever meet!

I am..

A Graphic Designer, A Web Developer, A Coder, Singer, Songwriter, Dancer, and SO much more.

I have been a freelancer for over 20 years. I started at a very long age creating digital art on Microsoft Paint. Knitting and Crocheting followed shortly after. Before I knew it, I was desiging and creating murals, business cards, t-shirt designs, and flyers.

My background is Haitian-American, but my 23 & Me states I'm mixed with just about a little bit of everything!

I was born and raised in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. I now live in Hollywood, Florida.

My dream is to become the greatest web developer and graphic designer. Every day I am always learning something new in order to make sure it happens.

I also hope to be able to teach others as well. I just know I have a lot to learn in the meantime before I get good enough to do so.

One day I hope to raise enough money to own a lounge where I can put on events and workshops that allows students to have opportunities to express their creativity.
